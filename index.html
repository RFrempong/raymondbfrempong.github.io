About Me

I am an Associate Professor of Development Economics. My research uses microdata and applied econometric methods to study food security, natural resources, inequality, child labour, health, and gender in Sub‑Saharan Africa. I aim to contribute to policy‑relevant work that informs both academic and public debates about development outcomes in low‑income contexts.

Research Interests

My primary research focuses on:

  • Development Economics
  • Food Security & Nutrition
  • Natural Resources & Inequality
  • Child Labour Economics
  • Gender & Intra‑Household Bargaining
