About Me

I am an Associate Professor (Maître de Conférences) in Development Economics at the European School of Political and Social Sciences, Catholic University of Lille.

My research uses microdata and applied econometric methods to study food security, natural resources, inequality, child labour, health, and gender in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Research Interests

  • Natural Resources and Inequality
  • Food Security and Nutrition
  • Child Labour and Household Decision-Making
  • Gender and Intra-Household Bargaining
